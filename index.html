~ Akan Names ~

~ What is Akan Naming ~

Akan names are derived from Ghanean culture.
Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a "day name"
which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.Enter your
birth date and gender in the form below to get to know your AKAN name.


~ How It works ~

Enter your Date of Birth, the month, as a number, and your year too, below.
Select your gender and click on the submit button.
You will get a pop up message showing you your
Akan Name.
